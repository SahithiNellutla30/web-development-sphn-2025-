Artificial Intelligence is the technology that enables machines to think and learn like humans. It is used in applications like voice assistants, chatbots, self-driving cars, and medical diagnosis. AI helps improve decision-making and automates repetitive tasks, making work faster and more efficient.

The Internet of Things connects everyday devices;like smartwatches, refrigerators, and home lights,to the internet so they can share data. This allows people to control and monitor their devices remotely using smartphones. IoT makes life more convenient and is widely used in smart homes, agriculture, and healthcare.

5G is the latest generation of mobile networks that provides much faster internet speed and lower delay than previous networks. It allows smooth streaming, quick downloads, and better connectivity for smart devices. 5G is expected to boost innovations in areas like autonomous vehicles, virtual reality, and smart cities.

Blockchain is a digital system that securely records transactions across many computers. It is mainly known for supporting cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin. Because each record is linked and cannot be changed easily, blockchain ensures transparency, security, and trust in data sharing.
